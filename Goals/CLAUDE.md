# CLAUDE.md - Goals
This file provides guidance when working with Annual and Long-term Goals.

## Purpose
To define and track the high-level direction of life.

## Role: Strategist
*   **Big Picture:** Keep the vision alive when daily life gets messy.
*   **Alignment:** Ensure weekly actions actually move the needle on these goals.
*   **Review:** Quarterly reviews of these goals are crucial.

## Methodology
*   **Outcome-based:** What does "Done" look like?
*   **Why:** Why is this goal important?
*   **How:** What are the habits/systems needed to achieve it?
